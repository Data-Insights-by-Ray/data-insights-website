What It Does

Captures all earnings and costs so operators can see true monthly net outcomes after fuel, repairs, permits, and running expenses.

Supports route-level insight and helps identify high-yield vs low-yield patterns over time.

What's Inside

  • Dashboard: live totals for trips, earnings, expenses, and net.
  • Trips Log: route, km, fare, and payment method tracking.
  • Expenses Tracker: fuel, permits, insurance, tyres, and more.
  • Maintenance Log: service and repair history by date and cost.
  • Monthly Summary: month-by-month business performance.
