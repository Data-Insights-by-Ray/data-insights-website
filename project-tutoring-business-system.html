What It Does

Supports full tutoring operations from enrollment through academic reporting and payment follow-up.

Centralizes admin tasks so tutors can spend less time on spreadsheets and more time on learning outcomes.

What's Inside

  • Dashboard: active students, sessions, revenue, outstanding invoices.
  • Student Register: student details, subjects, and schedule references.
  • Lesson Log & Marks: attendance + academic history.
  • Payments & Invoicing: invoice status and outstanding balances.
  • Student Report Generator: ID-based progress output for parents.
  • Schedule & Parent Comms: timetable and communication audit trail.
