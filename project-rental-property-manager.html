What It Does

Track rental occupancy, monthly collections, overdue balances, and lease expiries before they become compliance and cashflow issues.

Maintenance and expenses are categorized and carried into a tax-ready summary flow for SARS rental income submissions.

Dashboard keeps occupancy, expected rent, collected rent, and net income visible in real time.

What's Inside

  • Dashboard: occupancy, collections, outstanding balances.
  • Properties & Rooms: room-level rental setup and status.
  • Tenants & Leases: tenant records with lease expiry flags.
  • Payments Ledger: paid/overdue state and follow-up visibility.
  • Maintenance Log: repair cost tracking and completion status.
  • Expenses + Tax: Section 11 deduction categories and summary.
